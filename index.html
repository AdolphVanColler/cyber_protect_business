Product Overview

Cyber Protect Business Insurance offers protection against cyber emergencies including data breaches, hacking, malware, and cyber extortion. Our 24/7 Cyber Emergency Response Team provides expert support to restore operations quickly.

Coverage

  • Business Interruption Loss
  • Data Restoration Costs
  • Cyber Extortion Costs
  • Multimedia Liability Claims
  • Regulatory Expenses and Penalties
  • PCI Fines and Penalties
  • Funds Protect
  • Emergency Response Costs

Key Features

  • Simple application process
  • Low excess amounts
  • Customisable ratings
  • Retroactive cover
  • Multi-entity support
  • Third-party providers covered

Exclusions

  • Consumer protection or antitrust violations
  • Dishonest acts by the business
  • External service interruptions
  • Government confiscation
  • Physical property damage
  • Unlawful data collection
  • Contractual and professional liabilities

Who is this for?

  • SMEs using IT systems
  • Data-sensitive businesses
  • Online businesses
  • Any business with a bank account
