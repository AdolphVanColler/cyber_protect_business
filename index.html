Product Overview

Cyber Protect Business Insurance offers end-to-end protection for your business against cyber emergencies including data breaches, hacking, malware, and cyber extortion. Our 24/7 Cyber Emergency Response Team provides expert support to mitigate losses and restore operations quickly.

Coverage

  • Business Interruption Loss
  • Data Restoration Costs
  • Cyber Extortion Costs
  • Multimedia Liability Claims
  • Regulatory Expenses and Penalties
  • PCI Fines and Penalties
  • Funds Protect (fraudulent fund transfers)
  • Emergency Response Costs (forensics, legal, notifications, credit monitoring)

Key Features

  • Simple application process
  • Low excess amounts
  • Customisable ratings based on business needs
  • Retroactive cover available
  • Multiple entities covered under one policy
  • Third-party service providers included

Exclusions

  • Violation of consumer protection or antitrust laws
  • Intentional or dishonest acts by the business
  • Service interruptions outside business control
  • Government confiscation or damage
  • Physical property damage
  • Unlawful data collection
  • Contractual liabilities and professional service claims

Who is this for?

  • SMEs using computer systems
  • Businesses holding sensitive data
  • Online transacting businesses
  • Any business with a bank account
